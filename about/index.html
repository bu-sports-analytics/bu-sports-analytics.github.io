About Us

BUSAG is a club that unites sports and statistics lovers, catering to both recreational and professional interests by bringing our members together in projects, research, and fun events to make new connections. We host various professional and recreational events, like speaker panels, networking events, sports viewing parties, bracket-making, and field visits. We also provide a structured curriculum for members interested in learning more technical skills involved in sports analytics.
