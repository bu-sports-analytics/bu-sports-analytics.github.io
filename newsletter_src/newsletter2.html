Sports Headlines

BU Men’s Hockey Win Their 32nd Title In The 2025 Beanpot!

The Terriers defeated Boston College 4-1 during regulation in the Beanpot championship game on Monday to take home the trophy. Goals were scored by Svoboda (1), C. Huston (1), Eiserman (1), and McCarthy (1). Combined shot count totaled 88 shots, with freshman goalie Mikhail Yegorov putting stop to 43 of 44 shots faced. Yegorov won the Eberly trophy for highest save % (69/71) in the tournament and Cole Huston took home MVP for finishing the tournament with two assists and three goals.

This is BU’s first beanpot win since 2022.

Philadelphia Eagles Win Super Bowl LIX

In a rematch of Super Bowl LVII, the Eagles dominated the Kansas City Chiefs in a 40-22 win on Sunday in New Orleans and denied KC the three-peat. Jalen Hurts had 221 passing and 72 rushing yards, throwing two TDs and rushing for a third, earning him the MVP title. Patrick Mahomes and the Chief’s offense struggled, throwing a pick 6 to rookie Cooper DeJean and had six sacks allowed.

Jalen Hurts beat his previous record for most rushing yards by a QB (previous was 70) with a new record of 72 yds. Hurts is also the first QB to ever record 10+ passing and 10+ rushing touchdowns in a playoff career. Saquon Barkley broke the record for most rushing yards in a single season (including post season) with 2,504 yards.  

This was the Eagles second Super Bowl win in franchise history (first was in 2018).
